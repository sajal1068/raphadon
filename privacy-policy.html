Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026 • Learn how Raphadon collects, safeguards, and handles patient clinical and digital parameters securely.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and health-related parameters voluntarily submitted through our dynamic consultation booking interfaces, quote request systems, and contact forms. This dataset may encompass your full name, electronic mailing address, phone coordinates, geographical country coordinates, amputation types, and brief medical history logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your shared dataset parameters are processed exclusively to fulfill tailored clinical needs, including:

  • Generating accurate medical quotes and travel pipelines for international medical tourism setups.
  • Custom tailoring artificial limb sizing specifications and prosthetic/orthotic material requirements.
  • Dispatching requested technical brochures, resource catalogs, and clinic consultation status reports.

3. Strategic Network Data Sharing

Raphadon never sells or commercializes your sensitive details. To facilitate cross-border medical treatments seamlessly, relevant medical histories and conditions are shared safely only with our primary accredited international network hospital links (such as Artemis Hospital in India) and verified medical travel partners.

4. Advanced Information Protection

We implement solid physical, electronic, and administrative security layers—including secure hosting firewalls and database access restrictions—to lock data transmissions safely and prevent unauthorized data loss, exposure, or modification.

5. Your Rights & Access Controls

You maintain absolute ownership parameters over your personal profiles. You can contact our data administration desk at any time to inspect your collected information, request data modifications, or request a complete purge of your records from our systems database indexes.

Privacy Inquiries?

For deep details regarding our medical data policy rules or compliance layouts, connect with our administration officer.

Secure Encryption

All diagnostic reports and case notes files uploaded through our form pop-ups are locked securely and mapped to international medical travel security standards.
